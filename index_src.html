Our Story

NEW YORK COMES TO LAKELAND

What started as a love letter to the iconic halal carts of Manhattan has become Lakeland's most talked-about food truck. Owner Ali brought the authentic flavors of New York City street food to Central Florida — and hasn't looked back.

Every plate is prepared fresh with 100% halal-certified ingredients, bold spices, and that unmistakable NYC flair. From our signature saffron rice to our house-made white sauce, everything is crafted from scratch daily.
